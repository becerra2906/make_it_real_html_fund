Our Story

We are an independent coffee roasting company based in downtown Toronto. We specialise on single origin, small batch roasting, aiming to create the best possible cupping experience money can buy. We were set up by two colombian brothers who after living away from home for years, wanted a place where they could buy coffee with the standard and the knowledge expected by someone who grew in a country where coffee is everything.

Our Products or how we would say back home: nuestros productos

  • Green Coffee : coffee for you to roast at home. You can adapt it to your personal taste and need.
  • Roasted Coffee : our expertly roasted coffee is done in a manner that highlights the unique flavours of the varietal you bought, the area where the beans where grown and the washing/drying process.
  • Micro lot importing :we can help you import the microlots you require to trade only the best quality coffee. We are experienced in the management of producer country customs and importing/legalisation processes ensuring fast importing times.
